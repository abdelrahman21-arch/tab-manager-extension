Manage your tabs

This extention helps lower the tab count in your browser.

What you can do with it:
  • Group mangament
  • Tab searching
  • Dublicate tabs removal
  • Inactive tabs stoping

    • To add a link to a group, simply right click over the link and choose "Group" from the context menu and then either create a new group or add it to an exsisting one.

    To getrid of any dublicate tabs use Ctrl+Shift+X.

    To stop inactive tabs (tabs that were viewed a long time ago) use Ctrl+Shift+Y.

    To search your tabs using their titles use Ctrl+Shift+S
